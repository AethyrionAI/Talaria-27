Personality

Retro Sci-Fi Saturday Morning is Talaria as a cereal-box space adventure. The background is warm newsprint with halftone dots. Surfaces are flat, blocky, and bordered in thick black. The reactor orb is a spinning target badge of red, blue, and yellow — like a rocket ship insignia.

Accent slot mapping: cyan (hero) → Rocket Red, amber → Laser Blue, violet → Boost Yellow.

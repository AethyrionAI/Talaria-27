Personality

Graffiti Galaxy is Talaria after dark in a city that orbits Jupiter. The background is a deep purple-black void crossed by angular streaks of neon paint. Surfaces are raw and slightly tagged — clipped corners, dashed borders, stencil-punched labels. The reactor orb is a glowing spray cap spitting citron, violet, and hot-pink rings.

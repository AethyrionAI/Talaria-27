Personality

Haunted VHS is Talaria taped off late-night television. The interface is a warm CRT void wrapped in scanlines — phosphor-green telemetry burns through drifting static while magenta and cyan chroma ghosts bleed off every edge. The tracking bar never quite settles, and honestly, it's happier that way.

Theme Personality

Disco Inferno is Talaria at its most glamorous and dangerous: a mirror-ball dance floor that’s slowly catching fire. The background is a deep, smoky burgundy-black, lit by molten gold and flickering crimson. The accent slots are remapped — the canonical "cyan" slot becomes Disco Gold (the hero), "amber" becomes Mirror Silver, and "violet" becomes Hellfire Crimson. The display type is set in elegant Georgia italic, evoking a vintage nightclub marquee, while the body stays clean in SF Pro Text. Glow is cranked above 1.0, but the motion stays slow and photosafe to keep the chat readable. The reactor orb reads like a spinning disco ball: a bright gold core, concentric dashed rings, and a slow diamond "coin" halo catching the light. Danger is a searing red-orange that pops against the dark field without vanishing into the flames.

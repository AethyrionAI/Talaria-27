Personality

Deep Sea Diner is a retro fish-and-chips counter perched on the abyssal plain. The background is ink-black ocean pressure with cyan, coral, and gold glows drifting through the dark. Surfaces are glossy and reflective like a submarine mess hall. The reactor orb is a little anglerfish lure — cyan, coral, and gold rings pulsing in the depths.

Accent slot mapping: cyan (hero) → Lure Cyan, amber → Coral Diner, violet → Abyss Gold.

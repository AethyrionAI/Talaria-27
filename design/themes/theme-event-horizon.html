Personality

Event Horizon is Talaria at the edge of a black hole: a deep void-black interface lit by the violent glow of infalling matter. The background is an abyssal purple-black, streaked with drifting lensed starlight. Surfaces feel like the control panel of an X-ray observatory — soft violet rims, cyan telemetry, gold alert markers.

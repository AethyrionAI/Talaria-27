Personality

Glitch Garden is Talaria after dark in a greenhouse built from broken monitors. The background is pure dead-black with a faint green grid and CRT scanlines. Surfaces are glassy, slightly corrupted, and pulsing with neon green life. The reactor orb is a seed core wrapped in vine rings of green, cyan, and magenta.

Accent slot mapping: cyan (hero) → Vine Green, amber → Corruption Cyan, violet → Glitch Magenta.
